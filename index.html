Recent Work

Luxa4Slack

My team at work uses Luxafor Lights (pictured above) to indicate our work status to our coworkers. For example, green means we are available to answer questions, red means we should not be disturbed and yellow means we are away. We also use our lights to indicate when we have received instant messages. We recently switched from using Pidgin as our primary interoffice communication to Slack. So I decided to write an Electron app that integrates our local Luxafor statuses with Slack.
