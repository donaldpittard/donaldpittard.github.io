Recent Work

Luxa4Slack

 My team at work uses Luxafor Lights to indicate our work status to our coworkers. For example, green means we are available to answer questions, red means we should not be disturbed and yellow means we are away. We also use our lights to indicate when we have received instant messages. We recently switched from using Pidgin as our primary interoffice communication to Slack. So I decided to write an Electron app that integrates our local Luxafor statuses with our team's Slack.

Steph's Notes

 A Simple experiment to learn React. It started out as a simple idea: tell Alexa to take a note and I can view it somewhere like a digital display or online. Easy enough. I prototyped a version with Python running on RaspberryPi. And now I'm in the process of creating a full web application.
