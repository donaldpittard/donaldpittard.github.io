Projects

Luxa4Slack

 My team at work used Luxafor Lights to indicate our work status to our coworkers. For example, green meant we were available to answer questions, red meant we should not be disturbed and yellow meant we were away. We also used our lights to indicate when we have received instant messages. We recently switched from using Pidgin as our primary inter-office communication app to Slack. So I decided to write an Electron app that integrated our local Luxafor statuses with our team's Slack.

Steph's Notes

 This started as a simple idea from my girlfriend. She wanted a way to tell Alexa to take a note for her, and have the note displayed somewhere, almost like a voice-activated sticky notes. I built the prototype out using Python, Flask and a Raspbery Pi. You can view the code for the prototype here. Now that I have the prototype working, I am using this as a project to learn React.
